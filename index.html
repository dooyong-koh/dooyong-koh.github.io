Welcome!

Hi there! This is Dooyong Koh, a Ph.D. student in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Massachusetts Institute of Technology. I work on spintronic devices for probabilistic computing with Professor Marc Baldo and Professor Luqiao Liu.

Before joining these groups, I received my Bachelor's degree with a double major in Materials Science and Engineering and Electrical and Computer Engineering at Seoul National University. During the undergraduate years, I worked on Spiking Neural Networks and neuromorphic systems with Professor SangBum Kim and Professor Jong-Ho Lee.

My research interest broadly lies on emerging devices and systems for future computing technologies.
