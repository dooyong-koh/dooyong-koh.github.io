Welcome

Hi there! This is Dooyong Koh, a first-year PhD student in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Massachusetts Institute of Technology. I work on probabilistic computing using spintronic p-bits with Professor Marc Baldo and Professor Luqiao Liu.

Prior to MIT, I received my Bachelor's degree from Seoul National University with a double major in Materials Science and Engineering and Electrical and Computer Engineering. During the undergraduate years, I worked on Spiking Neural Networks and neuromorphic systems with Professor Sangbum Kim and Professor Jong-Ho Lee.

My research interest broadly lies on emerging devices and systems for future computing technologies.
